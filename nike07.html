Nike Metcon 8 By Sol Sigurdardottir

The Nike Metcon 8 NBY brings a touch of style to your training routine with chrome options on the Swoosh, heel plate and laces. Our refreshed, sport-inspired color palette is paired with a marble effect option along the outsole and sides. Go for a look that makes you stand out in the gym or transform your Metcon 8 with the colors that define the athlete in you: it's up to you.

Color Shown: Multi-Colour/Multi-Colour Style: DV2285-900

Elasticity and response

Nike React foam offers a soft, responsive ride and a comfortable, cushioned, lightweight, springy base for cardio workouts and sprints.

Grip from all angles

Sticky rubber wraps the arch of your foot on the most intense rope climbs. The rubber pattern provides traction and foot support as you go from the gym floor to the asphalt.

Stability for weightlifting

The wide flat heel adds even more stability with an inner plate that distributes weight from side to side. In addition, it is flexible to provide comfort in cardio exercises and sprints.
